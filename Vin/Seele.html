Seele is a quantum main dps unit who follows the path of the hunt

Seele is a hypercarry unit, which means that it is recommended to build a team around her damage output

Her best in slot relics are: 4pc quantum set, 2pc quantum and 2pc wild wheat.
As for planar ornaments, Herta Space Station and Salssoto have similar performance, but Rutilant Arena is the best if you reach the 70% crit rate requirement. Note that in the next versions we are going to get a new ornament from Penaconny, the new one should be her best one due to the speed requirement instead of crit rate.

For the lightcones, her best in slot is "In the Night," her signature lightcone

Seele's kit

Basic ATK: Thwack

Deals Quantum DMG equal to 100% of Seele's ATK to a single enemy.

Skill: Sheathed Blade

Increases Seele's SPD by 25% for 2 turns and deals Quantum DMG equal to 220% of Seele's ATK to a single enemy.

Talent: Resurgence

Enters the buffed state upon defeating an enemy with Basic ATK, Skill, or Ultimate, and receives an extra turn. While in the buffed state, the DMG of Seele's attacks increases by 80% for 1 turn. Enemies defeated in the extra turn provided by "Resurgence" will not trigger another "Resurgence."
Lacerate
While Seele is in the buffed state, her Quantum RES PEN increases by 20%.

Ultimate: Butterfly Flurry

Seele enters the buffed state and deals Quantum DMG equal to 425% of her ATK to a single enemy.
(video has audio)

Technique: Phantom Illusion

After using her Technique, Seele gains Stealth for 20 seconds. While Stealth is active, Seele cannot be detected by enemies. And when entering battle by attacking enemies, Seele will immediately enter the buffed state.

Notes

Using seele , is recommended to kill weaker enemies , so you can hit the strongest enemy with her tallent "Resurgence" to deal extra damage.
Seele's best support is Bronya, because of her buffs and action advance
