Bronya is a wind support unity who follows the path of harmony

Bronya is a offensive-support unit , wich means that her focus is to improve the team damage output through buffs or debuffs

Bronya doesn't has a best in slot relic set , she can pick 4pc wind set , 4pc wild wheat , 4 pc hackerspace or 2pc of any of those with 2pc knight of purity
As for planar ornaments , bronya can use Fleet of the ageless or Broken keel to further buff her temmates

As for lightcones , her best in slot is "But the battle ins't over" her signature lightcone , she can also use "past and future" as an alternative

Bronya's kit

Basic ATK: Windrider Bullet

Deals Wind DMG equal to 100% of Bronya's ATK to a single enemy.
Command
The CRIT Rate for Basic ATK increases to 100%.

Skill: Combat Redeployment

Dispels a debuff from a single ally, allows them to immediately take action, and increases their DMG by 66% for 1 turns.
When this Skill is used on Bronya herself, she cannot immediately take action again.

Talent: Leading the Way

After using her Basic ATK, Bronya's next action will be Advanced Forward by 30%.
Battlefield
At the start of the battle, all allies' DEF increases by 20% for 2 turns.
Military Might
When Bronya is on the field, all allies deal 10% more DMG.

Ultimate: The Belobog March

Increases the ATK of all allies by 55%, and increases their CRIT DMG equal to 16.0% of Bronya's CRIT DMG plus 20.0% for 2 turns

Technique: Banner of Command

After using Bronya's Technique, at the start of the next battle, all allies' ATK increases by 15% for 2 turns
